🌍 About the Rahmah Turkana Project

The Turkana region in Kenya has long faced severe drought, hunger, and spiritual neglect. Rahmah Turkana is a community-driven Islamic initiative focused on sharing authentic Hadiths daily and supporting the physical and spiritual upliftment of the people of Turkana.

Through this app, users receive daily Hadiths as a source of guidance and can support the project by donating as part of their Sadaqah Jariyah.
